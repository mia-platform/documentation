_December 11th, 2025_

:::info

Mia-Platform Console v14.4.1 is **now in Preview** and will be generally available on January 08th.

Console SaaS users can try out v14.4.1 latest improvements in Preview! Open a Service Request to ask for the creation of a sandbox Company in case you do not have access to any Company.

For self-hosted installations, please read the [following guidelines](#how-to-update-your-console).
:::

## How to update your Console

For self-hosted installations, please head to the [self hosted upgrade guide](/docs/infrastructure/self-hosted/installation-chart/how-to-upgrade) or contact your Mia-Platform referent and upgrade to _Console Helm Chart_ `v15.0.6-beta.0`.
