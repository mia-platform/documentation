## [v15.0.0 - Preview](/release-notes/v15.0.0.mdx)
*May 26th, 2026*

Mia-Platform v15 expands the platform with **three brand-new components**: **Context Catalog**, the single place to record, classify, and govern any entity that exists in your organization, with a built-in compliance layer and a growing set of connectors; **Flow**, a visual workflow orchestrator to design, execute, and monitor end-to-end processes across services, data pipelines, and human-in-the-loop steps; and **AI Foundry**, the new home for building, deploying, and governing AI-powered experiences, with agents, models, prompts, tools, skills, playbooks, and a live AI Playground. The release also introduces a dedicated **Charts** section in the Console Design Area for managing Helm chart configurations alongside the existing design and deploy workflows.
