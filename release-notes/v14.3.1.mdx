:::info

Mia-Platform Console v14.3.1 is **now in Preview** and will be generally available on October 30th.

For self-hosted installations, please head to the [self hosted upgrade guide](/docs/infrastructure/self-hosted/installation-chart/how-to-upgrade) or contact your Mia-Platform referent and upgrade to _Console Helm Chart_ `v15.0.4-beta.0`.
